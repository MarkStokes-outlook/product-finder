Coverage Analytics

Rates, not raw counts — answers "which source actually finds useful deals" rather than "which returns the most listings". Deal rate and duplicate suppression only count a source's primary sightings, so a cross-source duplicate can't inflate either figure. Average lifetime only includes listings that have actually ended or gone stale (48h unseen) — still-live listings are excluded because their lifetime hasn't concluded yet, and including it would understate the average and drift it every cycle.
